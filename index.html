Welcome to
Eubuleus
Eubuleus is a TTRPG (Tabletop Roleplaying Game) created by MangoChaton to bring the fun, interactive experience of games like Dungeons & Dragons to a completely different, unusual setting, that combines the genres of Sci-Fi, Fantasy, and Cyberpunk into one. The story as you see it is how events unfold in the game itself, which is acted out by MangoChaton and his friends.
The gist of the game is as follows:
 
The story follows a rebellion group, who live in the slums of a city named Band, which is located on an artificial island off of Japan, in the year 2873.
The government, also known as IGSOL (Intergalactic System of Order and Law), has decreed for a long while now that all magic users must be killed. No one is shown mercy. All who display any sort of magical ability, or who are reported to have magic, are slaughtered with extreme prejudice, regardless of age, social class, gender, race, or religion. Nothing matters, other than the fact they have magic.
The rebels, mostly consisting of magic users, have been trying to overthrow their oppressors for years. All attempts have failed, and many have lost hope. Except…
A ragtag rebel group, led by the mysterious Spade, plans on fighting the same fight that has been fought for years. They will never feel at peace until they enact justice. As odd (and perhaps even incompetent) as this group may seem, they have had more success than any rebel group before them.
